FRUITS

Definition:

A Fruit is a product of the tree that bears seeds and is consumed as a food for its sweet, edible flesh. They can be eaten in a raw form as a whole or cooked into a stew, juiced, diced or even dried and frozen forms. Technically, even avocadoes, tomatoes, cucumbers, squash and other sweet-tasting vegetables can also be treated as fruits.Fruits are important sources of dietary fibre, vitamins (especially vitamin C), and antioxidants. Although freshfruits are subject to spoilage, their shelf life can be extended by refrigeration or by the removal of oxygen from their storage or packaging containers. Fruits can be processed into juices, jams, and jellies and preserved by dehydration, canning, fermentation, and pickling.Fruit is high fibre content is one of its main advantages. It aids in the reduction of blood cholesterol levels. That brings us back to the first point: it reduces your chance of heart disease. Fibre is also extremely important for intestinal health. It keeps you “regular,” which can help prevent constipation, haemorrhoids, and diverticulosis. Foods high in fibre and low in calories can also help you lose weight (or keep it off) by making you feel fuller for longer. They also regulate your blood sugar, which allows you to eat less junk throughout the day.
